The Practice: Maxwell Counseling Associates specializes in helping families from diverse backgrounds in neurodiversity get specialized and integrative care. Our aim at Maxwell counseling and associates is to provide a inclusive format for counseling that provokes lasting change. In combination of psychotherapy and somatic skills, providers within this practice aim to promote self-awareness, self regulation, as well as internal reconciliation of the psyche and emotional body. We offer services remotely in the state of Texas, with in person sessions available in Coppell TX.

My background is extensive, having worked in every therapuetic millieu one can work in..... Hospital, ACT Team, School System, Intensive Outpatient Program. I understand the coordination of care and resource management.

[humans have layers]

I grew up in little Forest Hills of the DFW. I attended Booker T Washington, where I had a focus on ceramics, a passion I continue to enjoy to this day.

In 2005, I moved to New Orleans LA to obtain my bachelor's in psychology, which is where I first began working with kiddos on the autistic spectrum.

Following graduating from Loyola in 2009, I became more focused on my own psychological well-being and started studying Kripalu Yoga. I received my Kripalu teaching training certificate in 2010 and began teaching in the New Orleans area shortly after.

In 2012, I decided that I wanted to attend Graduate School to pursue research on using yoga in forms of rehabilitation and graduated from LSU with a master's in health science and a focus in clinical rehabilitation and counseling in 2015.

I started working in 'inpatient care' shortly after school, and in 'long term' as well as 'acute inpatient care'. I have worked on a assertive community treatment team, as well as within the school system.

In 2020, in the wake of the pandemic, I decided to specialize more in eating disorders- an adjacent to the trauma work that I was doing in private practice. I worked in Eating Recovery Centers IOP program and became licensed in Texas in 2021.

In 2023, I made the decision to move to be closer to my stepmother and father- whom I am very close to. I am very excited to be able to work in person in the city of Coppell serving DFW residents. When I am not being a therapist..., I am the mom to the most incredible child. And when I'm not doing that- I can be found either with my family, working out, practicing yoga, or being out in nature or my studio.

Services

Individual Session

Here, in this session, the therapist meets individually with the therapist. The Client and therapist cultivate therapeutic rapport, and further an individual treatment plan, to support.

Group Session

Whether it's a family of four, or two partners in a marriage, group counseling focuses on the dynamic of the individuals sharing in an experience. Here, in these sessions, the relationship(s) itself is receiving treatment, whether that's to make a plan for change or seek reconciliation for emotional repair.

Exercise Format

Here, in this session, the goal is to move stagnant emotional sensations through the emotional cycle to gain regulation.

Parental Coaching Sessions

Here in this session, the goal is to guide parents in facilitating attachment to further their child's growth.
